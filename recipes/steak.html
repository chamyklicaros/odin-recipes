Steak

A perfect steak for any person out there

Ingredients

  • ribeye steak
  • 1 clove garlic
  • half of a butter
  • rosemary and thyme

Steps

  1. put in the beef
  2. put butter
  3. chopped up garlic
  4. when butter is melted put in the steak
  5. Put in the herbs
  6. baste the steak with the butter
  7. cook for 2 - 4 minutes per side
  8. Remove the steak from the pan
  9. rest for 10 minutes
