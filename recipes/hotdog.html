Hotdogs

The classic tenderjuice hotdog

Ingredients

  • A pack of Tenderjuicy Hotdogs
  • A cup of water
  • a cup of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Put hotdogs in a preheated pan
  2. Pour in the Water
  3. When water has evaporated
  4. Pour in oil
  5. cook for another 1 minutes rotating the hotdog every few seconds
