Caldereta

Is a hearty beef tomato based dish from the Philippines

Ingredients

  • 2 pound beef
  • 1 liter of water or beef stock
  • vegetable oil
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 tomato sauce from a can
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

Steps

  1. chop up the vegetable
  2. heat up pan
  3. put oil on pan
  4. put the chop vegetable on the pan
  5. stir for 2 minutes then remove from pan
  6. put in the beef
  7. brown the beef
  8. pour in your chosen liquid
  9. slow cook for 1 hours
  10. on the 30 minutes put in your vegetable and tomato sauce
  11. afterwards put in the soy sauce and vinegar
  12. season with salt and pepper to taste
